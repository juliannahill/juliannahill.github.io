Publications

Newsletter: In the Age of 'Despacito,' It's Never Too Late to Learn a Foreign Language

This article is written from the intersection of foreign language education and popular culture. Establishing the popularity of Louis Fonsi and Daddy Yankee's music as an important and timely cultural artifact, I offer insight into how knowledge of foreign language permeates into our lives as global citizens. Don't miss the simple steps you can take to begin learning a foreign language on your own.

Video: Edumacation's Video Series

Produced and edited by Madison Stagner, Daniel Han, Niko Milosevic and myself, this video series of "Tip Clips" seeks to provide incoming freshman at Virginia Tech prelimenary knowledge about college life in Blacksburg. From checking out a library book, navigating the public bus system, to finding a hobby, our channel collects advice we wish we had when were first stepped foot on campus.

Podcast: An Educated Life Presents

In this informative and at times critical discussion about liberal education, Daniel, Madison, Niko and I illuminate the need for universities to revisit the way they enforce antiquated general education requirements in an increasingly advanced world. Using the newly proposed "Pathways to General Education" at Virignia Tech and an interview with Dr. Annie Hesp as our window into this issue, we imbue our group conversation with individual experience.
